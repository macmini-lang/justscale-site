506(b) Relationship Capital Advantage

Osprey's 506(b) structure allows for unlimited accredited investors through existing relationships, creating a significant fundraising advantage over 506(c) funds limited to general solicitation. This structure enables deeper due diligence conversations and relationship-building that institutional partners prefer. The regulatory framework supports a more consultative fundraising approach.

25-40% faster fundraising timeline SEC data shows 506(b) funds average 18-month fundraising cycles vs 24-30 months for 506(c) funds (SEC Private Fund Statistics, 2024)
