Questions, answered

Who is this training for?
Two groups: people who want to work in commercial real estate (analyst and associate paths at investment, development, and brokerage firms), and people who want to buy commercial property themselves. The framework applies to both, and Justin shows you how to pick your route.
Do I need a finance background or prior experience?
No. The training starts from the underwriting fundamentals and builds up. If you've never modeled a deal, you'll still be able to follow along and know your next step by the end.
Is it really free?
Yes. The live training is free to attend. Register with your name and email and you're in.
How long is it?
90 minutes, including live Q&A at the end. Block the full time so you can ask your questions and get them answered on the spot.
Will it be recorded?
This is a live-only session and no public replay is sent out. The most valuable parts, including the live deal walkthrough and Q&A, happen in the room. Show up live.
What do I need to attend?
A quiet 90 minutes, something to take notes with, and one specific question about your CRE situation. If you have a deal or a job listing you're looking at, bring it.
