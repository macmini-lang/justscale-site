The Practice

A Naples practice that thinks globally — and transacts with the precision of the zip code it represents.

Chris Resop founded The Resop Team on the premise that luxury real estate in Naples deserves a representation model built for the buyer pool it actually serves — a buyer pool that lives in New York, Chicago, Los Angeles, and London and moves between three or four residences each year. Since 2015, the team has closed more than $2 billion in Naples transactions, anchored by deep specialization in Port Royal, Grey Oaks, Old Naples, and Pine Ridge.

The decision to operate under The Agency amplifies that thesis. The Agency's offices in more than one hundred markets, its curated listing presentation, and its editorial-grade marketing are structurally aligned with the kind of property and client The Resop Team serves. The combination — Naples-specific fluency plus global reach — is what buyers and sellers at the $5M to $25M level actually need in 2026.
