The Principal

A quieter kind of expertise.

Patrick Dearborn has represented Naples' most discerning buyers and sellers since 2004 — closing nearly $1 billion in career sales across Old Naples, Port Royal, Pelican Bay, and Aqualane Shores. His work is defined less by volume than by discretion: the ability to move high-stakes transactions forward quietly, with the institutional knowledge to value a rare Gordon Drive lot, a tear-down tucked inside a 34102 zip code, or an estate that has never touched the open market.

The team operates as a boutique within John R. Wood Properties, the exclusive Christie's International Real Estate affiliate for Southwest Florida. Clients receive direct access to Patrick, an international marketing reach shared with the most exclusive properties in the world, and a depth of relationships across Naples that can only be earned across two decades in a market this specific.

"Every property in Naples has a story. Our work is telling it to precisely the right audience." — Patrick Dearborn
