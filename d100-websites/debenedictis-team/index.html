A Feature on the Principal

The case for a team built entirely inside the gates.

By The Editors · Naples, FL

Monika DeBenedictis built her Naples real estate practice on a single conviction: that the finest North Naples communities — Pelican Marsh, Mediterra, Quail West — are worlds unto themselves, and that the only way to represent them well is to live and work inside them. Twenty years later, the team she leads closes more than $80 million a year across those gates, with a client list that has quietly become a referral engine of its own.

The team operates under John R. Wood Properties, the exclusive Southwest Florida affiliate of Christie's International Real Estate, giving every listing the weight of a global luxury network. But the work itself — the knowing which Mediterra villa traded off-market last season, which Quail West lot backs onto the wrong lake for reliable resale, which Pelican Marsh buyer is quietly looking to upgrade — that remains, as it should, deeply local.

"The best real estate in North Naples never touches the MLS twice. It moves between the families who know the communities — and the agents who earned their seat at that table." — Monika DeBenedictis, Team Principal

What follows is a snapshot of the markets the team represents, the properties it is currently bringing forward, and the clients who have returned for second and third transactions across a career now twenty years in.
