The Principals

Karyn & Rowan Samuel.

A true two-principal team is rare. Two principals who genuinely work every transaction together — that is rarer still.

Karyn and Rowan Samuel represent the Samuel Team as a true partnership. Every client works directly with both of them. That structure is deliberate: it gives sellers two minds on pricing and strategy, it gives buyers a backup on every showing and every negotiation, and it gives both sides the kind of always-on responsiveness that a luxury transaction in Naples often demands. It also tends to compound — their book is heavily weighted toward repeat clients and the referrals those clients make.

The team operates under John R. Wood Properties, the exclusive Christie's International Real Estate affiliate for Southwest Florida, and was recognized with Christie's Masters Circle distinction in 2024 — placing the practice among the highest-producing advisors in the Christie's network worldwide. That recognition matters most for what it signals to the market: when Karyn and Rowan bring a listing forward, it travels through the same international channels as the most coveted properties on earth.

The day-to-day work remains specific and local. Old Naples. Park Shore. Estate homes within walking distance of 5th Avenue. Towers along Gulf Shore Boulevard. The neighborhoods where one or two well-placed conversations move more real estate than any open-house weekend ever has.
