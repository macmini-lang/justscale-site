01 — About the team

Two decades of Marco Island. Nothing to prove.

Michelle Thomas built this team the long way — one waterfront estate, one Hideaway closing, one Gulf-front condominium at a time. Over two decades the team has become the most productive luxury group on Marco Island and one of the top mega teams in the United States, closing approximately $380 million in 2023 alone.

What that volume really represents is relationships. Families who bought their first Marco condo in 2009 and came back a decade later for the beach house. Out-of-state clients who found the team on a referral from a neighbor on Hideaway and stayed because the representation was quieter, more technical, and more discreet than what the national brokerages were offering. The work is listing-side, buyer-side, and increasingly off-market — because at the top of this market, the best properties never see the MLS.
