The Brokerage

A different kind of Naples firm.

Gulf Coast International Properties was built on a simple premise: the Naples luxury market deserves representation that moves at the speed of relationships, not corporate policy. Since its founding, GCIP has operated as an independent brokerage — unburdened by national brand mandates, franchise fees, or centralized listing protocols that often do not serve the unique contours of our clientele.

The result is a firm whose agents can say yes, no, and not yet on behalf of their clients, with the full authority and discretion required at the $5M, $15M, and $40M+ transaction levels. Our deep bench of local knowledge — neighborhood history, architect provenance, waterfront rights, dock depth, seawall age, club membership transferability — is what you should expect from the firm that closes more Naples waterfront estates than any independent brokerage on the coast.

Tim Savage
Founder & Principal, GCIP
