The Partnership

Witthoff + Needles.

Two decades on the same zip code, two agents who know every private community gate on the island.

Stacy Witthoff
Co-Principal
Scott Needles
Co-Principal

Stacy Witthoff and Scott Needles built The Needles Group on the idea that Marco Island deserves the same caliber of representation as Naples proper — and, more pointedly, that the two markets require fundamentally different expertise. Marco is an island. The inventory, the buyer profile, the community dynamics, the beach-vs.-bay-vs.-Gulf question — all of it operates by different rules, and all of it is what the team has concentrated on for more than two decades.

The results speak to the thesis. The Needles Group closed 2024 as the #1 ranked team on Marco Island and has remained among the top three teams in John R. Wood Properties' entire Southwest Florida network from 2022 through 2024 — a network that spans more than twenty offices. The team operates under John R. Wood's exclusive affiliation with Christie's International Real Estate, connecting Marco sellers to a global luxury buyer network and giving buyers access to the kind of off-market inventory that, on an island this size, moves quietly between known brokers.

Areas of Expertise

The island, neighborhood by neighborhood.

Each Marco community operates as its own market. Here is how the team reads each of them.

Hideaway Beach

Private · Gated
Marco's premier private community at the north tip of the island. Gated, with its own beach, golf course, marina, and tennis center. Single-family estates and mid-rise residences.
Typical Range
$4M – $18M

Tigertail Beach

Beachfront
Beachfront and beach-adjacent residences in the northwest corner of the island — some of the most sought-after Gulf-front blocks outside a gated community.
Typical Range
$3M – $15M

Key Marco

Waterfront
Exclusive enclave on a separate key off Marco's northeast side. Private estates with direct Gulf-access water frontage, large lots, boutique scale.
Typical Range
$5M – $25M

Old Marco

Historic Core
The island's original settlement and its most walkable neighborhood. Mix of renovated historic cottages, bayfront estates, and new-construction infill.
Typical Range
$2M – $12M
