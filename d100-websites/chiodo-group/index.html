The Principal

Ryan Chiodo.

Ryan Chiodo does not compete on volume. He competes on fit, fluency, and the kind of relationship bank that has taken two decades of Naples coastal practice to accumulate. The numbers are a consequence: $1B+ in career transaction volume, a sustained position atop Compass's Southwest Florida Premium division, and a book of Port Royal, Aqualane, and Pine Ridge clients who transact, on average, every seven years.

The Chiodo Group's practice is structured around a single discipline: representing the buyer or seller whose transaction genuinely requires a specialist. That means properties whose pricing cannot be pulled from an MLS sort, whose title histories involve multi-generational trusts, whose dockage and club transferability issues deserve a firm that has closed twenty similar files. When a $20M Olde Naples estate needs to change hands quietly, Ryan is the phone call. When a Port Royal buyer needs access to a listing that will never be marketed, the same.
