Meet Roxanne Jeske

A practice built on trust, compounded over twenty-eight years.

Roxanne Jeske leads the Jeske Heenan Team with twenty-eight years of specialization in the Naples luxury market. Her ranking in the top 1.5% of all U.S. real estate professionals by RealTrends is the product of a practice defined by repeat clients, generational referrals, and the kind of market fluency that can only be developed across multiple cycles.

The team represents buyers and sellers across the luxury corridor from Naples through Bonita Springs to Estero — with particular depth in estate homes, golf and beach communities, and the kind of seasonal second-home transactions that require a steady hand from offer through closing. Operating under John R. Wood Properties gives every listing the marketing weight of the Christie's International network, while the team itself remains small enough to return every call personally.
