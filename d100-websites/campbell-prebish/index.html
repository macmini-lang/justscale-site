01 — The partnership

Two principals. One specialty. No generalists on the team.

Campbell & Prebish is a deliberately small partnership inside William Raveis. Thomas Campbell and Richard Prebish II have built the practice around a single discipline — Port Royal, Aqualane Shores, Old Naples, and the Gordon Drive corridor — and have declined the kind of growth that would dilute focus. The result is a team that closes $237 million without a national brand campaign, an IDX portal, or any interest in volume for its own sake.

What Port Royal buyers and sellers pay for is judgment. The Port Royal Club queue, the inspection posture on a 1960s seawall, the narrow list of architects worth hiring for a rebuild, when to run a listing and when not to — the partnership has lived through enough cycles to give its clients a straight answer on every one of those questions. That experience is the product. The rest is just the work behind it.
