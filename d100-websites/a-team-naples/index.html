The Team

The team you want when the transaction needs to be right.

Anthony Dwyer has been closing Naples coastal estates since 1980. Across four decades, he has represented three generations of buyers in Port Royal, watched Pelican Bay go from greenfield to one of America's most coveted gated communities, and personally closed enough Park Shore and Moorings transactions to know which buildings have which HOA issues, which streets flood, which architects worked on which floor plates.

The A Team Naples is the consolidation of that 45-year career into a disciplined five-agent structure at Compass. The team size is deliberate: small enough that every client receives direct principal-level attention, large enough to cover every Naples coastal micro-market simultaneously. That is the combination Anthony built the team around, and it is what sets us apart from both solo practitioners and the larger production teams you encounter at other firms.
