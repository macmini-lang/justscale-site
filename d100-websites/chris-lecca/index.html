01 / About

Chris Lecca operates a deliberately narrow practice inside William Raveis' Naples–Gulf Shore office. The scope is a single zip — 34108 — and the three communities inside it: Vanderbilt Beach, Park Shore, and Pelican Bay. That tight focus is what has produced the numbers. 02 / Neighborhoods

Three communities. Three different price curves.

The 34108 market is not a monolith. Each community trades on its own amenity structure, tower inventory, and buyer profile — and the practice moves fluently between them.

01
Gulf-front · Tower-led

Vanderbilt Beach

The northern strip of Gulf-front towers — Regatta, Vanderbilt Towers, Le Dauphin. Tighter beach, lower tower density, better relative value per square foot than Park Shore.

Median condo: $2.4M · SFH range: $2.8M – $14M
02
Beachwalk · Premium

Park Shore

Gulf Shore Boulevard's tower corridor plus bayfront west-of-Crayton single-family. The beachwalk and the mature oak canopy keep this the market's pricing reference point.

Median condo: $3.1M · SFH range: $3.4M – $22M
03
Amenity community · Private beach

Pelican Bay

2,100 acres, tram-access private beach, two beachfront restaurants, and mandatory Foundation membership. The most amenity-dense luxury community in Naples.

Median SFH: $3.8M · Condo range: $950K – $11M
