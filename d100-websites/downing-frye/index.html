The Firm

A Naples institution, by design.

Built on independence, local ownership, and a roster of Naples' most established producers.

Downing-Frye has operated as an independent full-service brokerage in Southwest Florida for decades — long enough that the firm's name is woven into the infrastructure of the Naples real estate market itself. The company's production footprint — roughly $974 million in closed volume across 1,124 transactions over a trailing twelve months — places Downing-Frye among the largest brokerages operating in SWFL, franchise or otherwise.

What separates the firm is not scale alone. It is that the scale was built without selling equity to a national franchise. Downing-Frye agents keep a larger share of their production, operate inside a brokerage that actually answers the phone locally, and transact with clients who understand the difference between a firm headquartered in Naples and one headquartered in a regional hub three time zones away. For a seller deciding where to list an estate on Port Royal, or an agent deciding where to park their business, that independence is the point.

Vice President · General Manager

Mike Hughes leads operations and agent services for Downing-Frye, with oversight across the firm's Naples and Southwest Florida footprint. His role sits at the intersection of brokerage strategy and day-to-day support — the systems, tools, and structure that let top producers stay focused on the work.

Under his stewardship, Downing-Frye has continued to attract experienced agents looking for the advantages of a locally owned firm without the fee drag and policy layers of a franchise model. Mike's approach is hands-on, accessible, and built around the idea that a brokerage exists to serve its agents — not the other way around.
