The Principal

Four decades, one market.

A career built entirely in Naples, spanning multiple cycles and generations of ownership.

Karen Van Arsdale started her Naples career before the town had a Mercato, before Fifth Avenue South had returned, and before Port Royal had reset from its initial wave of 1970s construction into the market it is today. The benefit of that timeline is not nostalgia — it is data. Karen has personally represented properties through multiple full cycles in each of her core neighborhoods, which is the kind of history you cannot rebuild with a CRM or a marketing budget.

Today the team closes at the top of the Naples luxury market, with approximately $168 million in 2023 team volume and a career total that has crossed $2 billion. The work is concentrated in Old Naples, Port Royal, Park Shore, and Pelican Bay — with a long history of off-market transactions at the estate tier. Operating under Premier Sotheby's International Realty gives the practice the global marketing reach, private-client infrastructure, and Sotheby's network advantages that matter at the $10M+ tier.
