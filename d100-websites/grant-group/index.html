The Principal

Doug Grant.

A waterfront specialist in a town where the waterfront is the market.

Doug Grant built The Grant Group on the observation that Naples is not one real estate market but two — the waterfront market, which behaves like its own asset class, and everything else. He chose to specialize in the first. That discipline has produced a team consistently ranked inside the top twenty nationally, including a #17 ranking in the United States and approximately $184 million in closed volume in 2023 alone.

The waterfront is the Naples market. Everything else is context.

The group operates under John R. Wood Properties, the exclusive Southwest Florida affiliate of Christie's International Real Estate. The combination of local dockage expertise — fixed bridges, draft, slip economics, marina structure — with Christie's global buyer reach is what moves the most capital-intensive properties in Naples. From a single-slip canal lot to an 80-foot dock at Pelican Isle, the team has almost certainly represented a comparable transaction within the last twelve months.
