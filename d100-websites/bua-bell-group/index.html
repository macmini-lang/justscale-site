The Principal

Tade Bua-Bell.

Naples native. Luxury specialist. A reputation built on discretion as much as production.

Tade Bua-Bell founded the Bua-Bell Group on a simple premise: that the top tier of the Naples market rewards brokers who know the zip code block by block — the tear-down economics of a Gordon Drive lot, the club-transfer timeline of a Moorings cooperative, the off-market pipeline that never reaches a public MLS. That premise has produced more than $2.5 billion in career closed volume and a consistent ranking among the top 25 brokerage teams in all of Southwest Florida.

The group operates under John R. Wood Properties, the exclusive Southwest Florida affiliate of Christie's International Real Estate. That affiliation matters in both directions: sellers access a curated international buyer pool tied to the Christie's auction and collector network, and buyers access the off-market inventory that Naples' top producers quietly move between one another before anything is ever listed. The Bua-Bell Group works the 34102 and 34103 corridors with an intensity that is difficult to replicate.
