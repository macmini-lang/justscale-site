Mission

A platform built by the people who built the assets, not the spreadsheets.

Weinreb Ventures Holdings is a Dallas-based family office and multi-strategy investment platform. We deploy permanent capital and partner with select operators across real estate, hospitality, technology, and entertainment. We don't manage outside money. We don't chase fees. We invest in what we know, and we know how to build.

Founder & Chairman

David R. Weinreb has spent thirty years turning land into communities, and capital into compounding platforms.

As co-founder and CEO of The Howard Hughes Corporation from 2010 through 2019, David led the company from a ~$500M market cap to nearly $6B, building master-planned communities including Ward Village in Honolulu, the Las Vegas Ballpark, and The Woodlands in Houston.

Today he serves as Chairman of Dar Global (LSE: DAR), with roughly $23B in active development underway across global markets. In 2013 he was named Ernst & Young Entrepreneur of the Year in Real Estate, and serves on the national board of the Alzheimer's Drug Discovery Foundation.

Weinreb Ventures Holdings is the platform he built to deploy that experience, alongside a senior team drawn from Howard Hughes, Cushman & Wakefield, PIMCO, and Invitation Homes.
