About Renee

A practice built on quiet excellence.

Renee Hahn's practice is defined by what does not appear on her website: the off-market transactions, the private buyer introductions, the quiet negotiations for clients whose names never belong in a press release. In fifteen years on the Naples coast, she has built a Top 0.05% national ranking — not by chasing volume, but by becoming the agent that returning clients refer their most discreet friends to.

The Renee Hahn Group operates within Alfred Robbins Realty, a boutique firm with a William Raveis affiliation that provides global network reach without the mandate-heavy structure of a national franchise. The combination — personal practice, institutional reach — is what allows Renee to represent both a first-time Vanderbilt Beach buyer and a longtime Park Shore seller with the same standard of care.
