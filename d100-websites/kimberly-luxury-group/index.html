The Principal

A practice built on discretion and precision.

Kimberly Alvord has spent her career in Naples representing properties where the brief is always the same: discretion, precision, the right buyer.

The Kimberly Luxury Group was built around a simple belief: luxury real estate is a service business first and a transaction business second. The difference shows up in the small decisions — how a listing is presented, which buyers see it first, how an offer is structured, whether a seller's name ever appears in the MLS notes. These are not details. They are the work.

"At this price tier, the transaction is rarely the hard part. The relationship is."

Kimberly was named to Christie's International Real Estate Masters Circle in 2024 — a recognition reserved for the highest-producing agents across the Christie's global affiliate network. The team operates under John R. Wood Properties, Christie's exclusive Southwest Florida affiliate, which gives clients the combined benefit of a decades-established Naples firm and the international marketing reach of the Christie's brand.

Neighborhoods Represented

Four enclaves inside the 34102.

A tight geographic footprint, focused on Naples' highest-value residential addresses.

Old Naples

Historic walking blocks between 5th Avenue South and the Gulf. Walkable, scarce, and location-inelastic at the beachfront tier.

Typical Range$4M – $22M

Port Royal

The highest-value residential enclave in Naples. Deep-water access and club eligibility define both the ceiling and the expectations.

Typical Range$14M – $48M

Moorings

Mid-century coastal Naples. Beachfront condos, waterfront single-family, and a quieter alternative to Park Shore at a materially lower entry.

Typical Range$2.5M – $12M

Mile-long private beach, boulevard estates, and iconic beachfront towers. New-construction and legacy ownership in tension across the neighborhood.
