i — about

Every transaction runs through one person.

John Paul Prebish built the practice around a single premise: that in the ultra-luxury Naples market, the client is paying for one agent's judgment and should receive exactly that. There is no team layer between JP and the seller's architect. No associate handling the inspection. No separate showing agent. The person you meet on day one is the person standing at the closing table.

That posture has produced more than $200 million in annual closed volume in each of the 2019 and 2020 production years, across Olde Naples, Park Shore, Coquina Sands, and the Moorings. The practice is capped by design — the roster of simultaneous clients is small — which is what makes the single-principal promise structurally possible. Most of the work arrives by direct referral from a previous client or a neighbor on the same street.

ii — neighborhoods

Four streets of coastline. Each priced differently.

The practice works only the Gulf-adjacent communities between Central Avenue and Seagate Drive — a geographic footprint tight enough to know every block, every tower, every builder.

i.
Historic district

Olde Naples

The grid of historic cottages and discrete new-builds inside walking distance of Fifth Avenue, Third Street South, and the pier. Limited land. High demand.

Median $6.8M · Range $2.8M – $28M
ii.
Beachfront towers

Park Shore

Gulf Shore Boulevard's high-rise corridor plus bayfront single-family west of Crayton. The beachwalk is a meaningful quality-of-life differentiator.

Median $4.2M · Range $1.4M – $22M
iii.
Low-density · Quiet

Coquina Sands

The quiet neighbor to Park Shore. No tower inventory, lower density, and a residential street grid that trades at a modest discount — with some of the best walking access to the beach on the entire coast.

Median $3.6M · Range $1.6M – $14M
iv.
Deeded beach club

Moorings

The deeded beach club transfers with the home — private access that is not available for separate purchase. A material component of pricing and the reason many buyers choose the Moorings first.

Median $3.2M · Range $1.3M – $16M
