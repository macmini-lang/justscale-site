About Mari Vesci

Thirty-two years, one corridor.

Some agents sell across Naples. Mari Vesci has spent thirty-two years learning four neighborhoods along the Gulf exceptionally well.

Mari Vesci founded the Vesci Team in 1992, and in the three decades since, she has quietly become one of the longest-tenured luxury specialists in the Vanderbilt Beach and Pelican Bay corridor. Her practice is narrow by design: Vanderbilt Beach, Pelican Bay, Naples Cay, Moraya Bay, and the surrounding Gulf-front towers. That narrow focus — in a market where most agents spread themselves across twenty-plus neighborhoods — is precisely what makes the work valuable.

The team operates under John R. Wood Properties, the exclusive Christie's International Real Estate affiliate for Southwest Florida, and every listing additionally benefits from the marketing reach of Luxury Portfolio International and The Realty Alliance. The result is a practice small enough to remain personal, with distribution large enough to put every listing in front of the international luxury buyers it deserves.

Across three decades and multiple market cycles, Mari has guided two and three generations of the same family through Gulf-front purchases, sale transitions, and portfolio rebalances. That is not marketing language — it is what a thirty-two year practice along four neighborhoods actually produces.

"The best Gulf-front transaction I ever closed was the third home I had sold that same family. That is what longevity in this business actually looks like." Mari Vesci · Team Principal
