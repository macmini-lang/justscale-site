The Partnership

A father-and-son practice on the Naples coast.

Philip Collins has spent his career operating in the narrowest sliver of the Naples market — the beachfront, bayfront, and estate addresses that extend south from Old Naples through Port Royal and down the Gordon Drive corridor. That work is a different discipline from Naples real estate in the aggregate. It requires a trained eye for seawalls, setbacks, and the land-value math that determines whether a $12M house is actually a tear-down lot wearing a structure.

PJ Collins joined the practice after a decade of his own production, bringing the kind of contemporary marketing fluency and buyer network that today's luxury transactions require. The team now operates under William Raveis, pairing two generations of Naples-coast specificity with the firm's broader infrastructure — proprietary buyer tools, private-market channels, and the New England wealth referral pipeline that still drives a meaningful share of Naples' $5M+ activity.
