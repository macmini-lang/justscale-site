The Practice

A specialist model, in a generalist market.

Most Naples agents cover a broad geography and a broad price tier. Nicola Wakelin does something different. She built her book around a single gated community — Riverstone — studied it at a level most agents never reach on any neighborhood, and used that specialization as the foundation for broader North Naples relocation work.

The economics of that model are straightforward: in a community like Riverstone, where the buyer profile is tightly defined and the resale dynamic depends heavily on pricing precision, a specialist meaningfully outperforms a generalist. Sellers get a shorter timeline and a better number. Buyers get a guided tour through a community that looks deceptively similar from the outside and transacts at meaningfully different levels once you know what to look at.

Today the team operates at the JRW Diamond tier, with prior top 2-5% national recognition at a previous brokerage. Nicola represents properties across the broader North Naples luxury corridor — Mediterra, Talis Park, Grey Oaks, and Pelican Marsh — under the John R. Wood Properties | Christie's International Real Estate umbrella.

The Niche

Riverstone, end to end.

Riverstone is a 712-home gated community in North Naples' 34119 corridor — a market that rewards knowing the difference between the estate, executive, and classic floorplans, between the preferred lot positions and the ones the data says to avoid, and between pricing that moves and pricing that sits.

The Wakelin team has represented a disproportionate share of the community's top-tier closings — across both list-side and buy-side work — and maintains an active shadow inventory of owners considering sale that no general listing search will surface.
