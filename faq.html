Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about how JustScale works, who we work with, and what to expect from done-for-you paid advertising.

What does JustScale do?

JustScale is a done-for-you paid advertising agency. We build, run, and optimize paid ad campaigns on Meta, Google, and YouTube for three types of businesses: education companies (info products, courses, coaching), capital-raising firms (private funds, syndicators, GPs), and established local businesses.

We handle strategy, creative, media buying, landing pages, and reporting end-to-end.

Who is JustScale for?

JustScale is for established businesses ready to scale with paid advertising — typically education companies doing $30k+/month looking to break $100k/month, capital-raising firms with a track record needing predictable investor flow, and local service businesses wanting to dominate their market online.

We are not a fit for early-stage startups, businesses without a proven offer, or anyone looking for a quick fix.

What industries does JustScale work with?

We focus on three verticals: education and information products (online courses, coaching programs, sales training, masterminds), capital raising (private equity funds, real estate syndications, venture funds, hedge funds), and high-margin local businesses (auto care, home services, professional services, premium retail).

How does done-for-you marketing work at JustScale?

After an application call to confirm fit, we audit your offer, funnel, and existing data. We then build the campaign infrastructure — landing pages, ad creative, tracking, and audiences — and launch within 14 to 21 days.

Once live, we manage daily optimization, weekly creative testing, and monthly strategy reviews. You stay focused on fulfillment; we handle the marketing.

Who founded JustScale?

JustScale was founded by Zac Regan, an agency operator based in Denver, Colorado. Zac built JustScale to deliver a higher standard of done-for-you marketing for education businesses, capital-raising firms, and local market leaders.

How does JustScale scale education businesses?

For education businesses we build direct response funnels — typically VSL-to-application or webinar-to-call funnels — and drive cold traffic from Meta, Google, and YouTube. We focus on cost-per-application, show rates, and close rates as the leading indicators, not vanity metrics like CPM or CTR.

The goal is predictable monthly revenue past $100k/month.

How does JustScale help capital-raising firms?

For capital-raising firms we build investor pipelines that comply with 506(b) or 506(c) requirements depending on the offering. The system typically combines paid ads, a credibility-focused landing page or investor portal, and a nurture sequence that warms accredited investors before a discovery call.

The goal is consistent, qualified investor flow rather than one-off campaigns.

How does JustScale help local businesses?

For local businesses we combine a modern, conversion-focused website with paid ads on Google and Meta targeted to your service area. The objective is to dominate the top of the funnel in your local market — capturing high-intent searches, retargeting site visitors, and generating consistent inbound leads or bookings.

What results can I expect from JustScale?

Results depend on offer strength, market, and operational capacity. Typical milestones for education clients are first qualified applications within 14 days of launch and a path to $100k+/month within 90 days. Capital clients typically see qualified investor calls within the first 30 days.

Past results are not guarantees — outcomes depend on factors including effort, market conditions, and the quality of your underlying offer.

How do I start working with JustScale?

Apply at justscale.co/book. We review every application personally. If we are a fit you will be invited to a strategy call to map out your campaign.

We work with a limited number of clients per vertical to protect quality of delivery, so applications are reviewed in the order received.
