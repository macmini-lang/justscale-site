The Supply Chain Problem

The U.S. imports 87% of its antimony. China just restricted exports.

Antimony is in ammunition, flame retardants, night vision goggles, and next-generation EV batteries. It is not optional for the defense industry. It is not optional for battery manufacturers. And right now, almost all of it comes from one country that is actively restricting supply.

The Pentagon responded by adding antimony to the critical minerals list. The Defense Production Act now gives preferential contracts to domestic suppliers. That changes the math for every North American antimony producer.
