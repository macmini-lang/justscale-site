01 — Executive Summary

Apex Capital Partners has built a strong track record in multifamily real estate — $180M AUM, three funds, and consistent returns. 02 — Market Context

Where the market is moving.

The multifamily capital raising landscape shifted dramatically in 2024-2025. Funds that relied exclusively on broker-dealer networks and personal relationships saw raise timelines stretch from 6 months to 14+ months. Meanwhile, firms that built direct-to-investor digital channels — paid media, content, automated nurture — are completing raises 40-60% faster than network-dependent peers.

This isn't a theoretical trend. Meta Ad Library data shows a 3x increase in fund-related advertising from real estate sponsors since Q3 2024. The firms moving first are locking in lower CPAs and building investor lists that compound across future raises. The window for first-mover advantage in multifamily fund marketing is narrowing — but it's still open for firms with Apex's track record.

Investor Acquisition Funnel

A four-stage investor acquisition funnel designed to take an accredited investor from first ad impression to booked qualification call in 7-14 days. Every stage is designed to qualify and educate simultaneously — by the time someone books a call, they already understand the thesis.

Ad → Landing Page

  • Fund thesis video (60-90 sec, talking head)
  • Three key stats: AUM, track record IRR, current raise target
  • Single CTA: 'Get the Fund III Details'
  • Accreditation pre-qualifier (yes/no gate)
  • No navigation links — single-purpose page

Qualification Application

  • 5-field form: name, email, accreditation status, investment timeline, minimum check size
  • Progress bar showing step 2 of 3
  • Trust signals: SEC filing link, fund counsel name, no-obligation language
  • Auto-redirect to confirmation page on submit

Confirmation + Fund Materials

  • Immediate access to fund overview PDF or dedicated page
  • Track record summary with net returns by fund
  • Team bios with relevant credentials
  • Book a call CTA embedded inline (not a separate page)
  • Email sequence triggers automatically

Email Nurture → Call Booking

  • 4-email sequence over 14 days (see email assets above)
  • Each email drives to fund materials or call booking
  • Behavioral triggers: if they revisit the page, send a personalized follow-up
  • Calendar integration: Calendly with pre-populated investor info
