Preservation Fund III is raising $100M to preserve affordable housing across the U.S., building on their track record of 2,081 units preserved and $344M in AUM. With their recent $156M Fund 3 close and strategic acquisitions like the 85-unit Pine Berry Senior Apartments in Clearwater, they're positioned for significant expansion. Analysis

Preservation Fund III, LLP's current marketing approach appears to rely heavily on personal networks and direct outreach, with no evidence of systematic paid acquisition strategies. Their website highlights their commitment to preserving affordable housing, which is a strong narrative but lacks a broader digital marketing strategy to reach a wider audience. Compared to top-performing funds, they are missing out on leveraging digital channels like Meta for targeted campaigns and retargeting strategies to engage potential investors who have shown interest.
