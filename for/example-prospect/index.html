01 / The Bottom Line

Redstone has real deal flow and a solid track record. $220M AUM across two funds, consistent distributions, repeat LPs. But the investor acquisition side relies almost entirely on existing relationships and broker networks. There is no systematic way to reach the 19M+ accredited investors who don't already know the fund exists. This briefing maps the gaps and shows exactly what it would take to build a repeatable investor pipeline.

02 / Market Context

Where the market is moving.

Commercial real estate fund marketing changed in 2024-2025. Sponsors who depended on broker-dealer networks and personal introductions watched raise timelines stretch past 12 months. Firms that built direct-to-investor digital channels, paid media, video content, automated nurture, are closing raises 30-50% faster.

A Meta Ad Library search shows a sharp increase in fund-related advertising from CRE sponsors since Q3 2024. The firms moving first are building investor lists that compound across future raises. The window for early-mover advantage in commercial real estate fund marketing is still open for firms with a track record like Redstone's.

Investor Acquisition Funnel

A four-stage investor acquisition funnel designed to take an accredited investor from first ad impression to booked qualification call in 7-14 days. Every stage qualifies and educates simultaneously. By the time someone books a call, they already understand the thesis.

Ad to Landing Page

  • Fund thesis video (60-90 sec, talking head)
  • Three key stats: AUM, track record IRR, current raise target
  • Single CTA: 'Get the Fund II Details'
  • Accreditation pre-qualifier (yes/no gate)
  • No navigation links, single-purpose page

Qualification Application

  • 5-field form: name, email, accreditation status, investment timeline, minimum check size
  • Progress bar showing step 2 of 3
  • Trust signals: SEC filing link, fund counsel name, no-obligation language
  • Auto-redirect to confirmation page on submit

Confirmation + Fund Materials

  • Immediate access to fund overview PDF or dedicated page
  • Track record summary with net returns by fund
  • Team bios with relevant credentials
  • Book a call CTA embedded inline (not a separate page)
  • Email sequence triggers automatically

Email Nurture to Call Booking

  • 4-email sequence over 14 days (see email assets above)
  • Each email drives to fund materials or call booking
  • Behavioral triggers: if they revisit the page, send a personalized follow-up
  • Calendar integration: Calendly with pre-populated investor info
