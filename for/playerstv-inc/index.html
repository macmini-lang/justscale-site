Why Paid Ads vs. Traditional Methods

Paid acquisition doesn't replace your existing channels. It adds a new one on top. Here's how they compare side by side.

Paid Ads (JustScale)
Cost per qualified lead$120 to $300 per accredited investor who fills out your application
Cost per dollar of capital raisedFor every $1 you spend on ads, you can expect $10 to $50 in committed capital based on your fund's close rate
Time to first leadsFirst qualified applications within 2 to 4 weeks of launching campaigns
ScalabilityIncrease or decrease your ad budget at any time. More spend means more qualified applications
Placement Agent
Cost per qualified leadYou don't pay per lead. You pay 2 to 3% of all capital raised, whether it took 5 intros or 500
Cost per dollar of capital raised$0.02 to $0.03 per dollar raised, paid as a percentage of committed capital at close
Time to first leads3 to 6 months before the agent's network produces qualified investor conversations
ScalabilityLimited to the agent's existing relationships. When the rolodex runs out, the pipeline stops
In-House BD Team
Cost per qualified lead$150K+ per year in salary and benefits before a single lead is generated
Cost per dollar of capital raisedVaries wildly. A strong hire pays for themselves. A bad hire costs you a year of runway
Time to first leads6 to 12 months to hire, onboard, and ramp before consistent pipeline
ScalabilityEvery new market or channel requires another hire. Scales linearly with headcount
Warm Intros / Network
Cost per qualified leadFree. The best channel you have. Also the one with a hard ceiling
Cost per dollar of capital raised$0 in direct cost. But once your network is tapped, there's no way to add more
Time to first leadsImmediate. Your existing relationships are the fastest path to capital
ScalabilityCapped by who you already know. Paid ads are the complement that extends beyond your network

Sales Team Playbook

6-step process for converting paid leads into LP commitments
+
01
Lead Comes In
Accredited investor fills out the application from your Meta ad. You get notified immediately.
02
Speed to Lead
Call within 5 minutes. Not 5 hours. The first fund manager to call gets 80% of the commitments.
03
Qualify on First Call
Confirm accreditation status. Confirm investment timeline. Confirm liquidity. If they pass, book the presentation call.
04
Send Pre-Call Materials
Email the fund overview deck, PPM summary, and a short video from you within 1 hour of the qualify call.
05
Presentation Call
Walk them through the fund thesis, track record, deal pipeline, and fee structure. Answer questions. Don't pitch.
06
Follow-Up Sequence
If they don't commit on the call, they enter a 14-day email nurture. Each email teaches something new about the fund. Day 14 is a soft close.
