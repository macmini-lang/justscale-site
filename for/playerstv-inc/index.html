PlayersTV, Inc. appears to have limited digital marketing efforts, as indicated by the absence of a website and social media presence. In contrast, successful fundraising often involves systematic digital strategies, such as targeted advertising and retargeting, which PlayersTV, Inc. has yet to adopt. The lack of a digital footprint may hinder their ability to reach a broader audience and efficiently raise capital.
