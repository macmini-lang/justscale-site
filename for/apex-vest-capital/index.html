Sales Team Playbook

6-step process for converting paid leads into LP commitments
+
01
Lead Comes In
Accredited investor fills out the application from your Meta ad. You get notified immediately.
02
Speed to Lead
Call within 5 minutes. Not 5 hours. The first fund manager to call gets 80% of the commitments.
03
Qualify on First Call
Confirm accreditation status. Confirm investment timeline. Confirm liquidity. If they pass, book the presentation call.
04
Send Pre-Call Materials
Email the fund overview deck, PPM summary, and a short video from you within 1 hour of the qualify call.
05
Presentation Call
Walk them through the fund thesis, track record, deal pipeline, and fee structure. Answer questions. Don't pitch.
06
Follow-Up Sequence
If they don't commit on the call, they enter a 14-day email nurture. Each email teaches something new about the fund. Day 14 is a soft close.
