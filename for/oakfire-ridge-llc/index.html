Oakfire Ridge is raising $16M to build San Antonio's first luxury members-only club on 49 private acres, targeting the city's growing high-net-worth population. With only $100,000 raised to date from their February 2024 filing, they're positioned for significant member acquisition growth but likely need proven hospitality marketing systems to reach their affluent target demographic. The private club industry has shown resilience post-COVID, with membership demand outpacing supply in major Texas markets.
