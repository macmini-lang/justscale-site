# Confirmation Video Outline — Education

Film conversational, not scripted. Riff from these bullets. Two minutes max.

- Your call is booked. I want to make sure you get the most out of it.
- We only take on a handful of clients at any given time, so the time we have on this call matters. I want to spend it on your business, not catching you up on how we work.
- Before the call, know your numbers. Revenue, ad spend, cost per acquisition, close rate. The more specific you are, the more specific I can be about what's broken and what to do about it.
- Below this video there's our YouTube content. Watch as much as you can. Every video you watch is one less thing I have to explain on the call and one more minute we spend actually solving your problem.
- Under that, common questions people have before the call. If something's on your mind, it's probably answered in one of those videos.
- People who come in having watched this stuff get 10x more out of the call. That's just the reality.
- See you on the call.
