# Confirmation Video Outline — Capital Raising

Film conversational, not scripted. Riff from these bullets. Two minutes max.

- Your call is booked. I want to make sure you get the most out of it.
- We work with a very small number of funds at any given time, so the time we have on this call matters. I want to spend it on your fund, not catching you up on how we work.
- Before the call, know your numbers. Raise target, where your LP pipeline stands, your timeline, what you've already tried. The more specific you are, the more specific I can be about what's going to move the needle for your raise.
- Below this video there's our YouTube content on raising capital with paid ads. Watch it. Every video you watch is one less thing I have to explain and one more minute we spend on your specific fund.
- Under that, common questions fund managers have before the call. SEC compliance, placement agents, investor quality, all of it. Watch the ones that are relevant to your situation.
- Fund managers who come in having watched this stuff get 10x more out of the call. That's just the reality.
- See you on the call.
