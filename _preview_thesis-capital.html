Where You Win

Differentiated operator-led investment thesis

Thesis Capital builds a bench of seasoned operators first, then sources businesses to match their skills. This is the opposite of how most PE firms work and is a genuinely distinct positioning that resonates with sophisticated LPs.

Founder is himself an operator

Ian Reynolds, the founder, comes from an operating background. The whole firm is structured around the conviction that operational outperformance beats financial engineering, which is increasingly relevant in a compressed multiples environment.

Track record from prior fundless sponsor deals

Before launching the drawdown fund, Thesis ran fundless sponsor transactions through capital partners. Those individual deals built credibility with a specific set of LPs who can be re-approached for the new vehicle.

Where You Bleed Revenue

Website is built for sellers, not investors

Per Alan's own admission on the discovery call, the current Thesis site is heavily weighted toward acquisition targets (sellers of businesses), not toward LPs evaluating the fund. There is no investor-facing section, no clear path to the deck or teaser, and no application flow.

Every accredited investor or family office who finds the site has nowhere to convert. Estimated 20-40 LP conversations per year lost to a non-existent funnel.

No paid acquisition infrastructure

Zero Meta or Google ad campaigns detected via Ad Library and Transparency Center. Current pipeline depends on cold outreach to RIAs and a planned placement agent engagement in June.

Estimated $5M-$15M in first-fund LP capital not reached during the active raise window

No automated email nurture

Thesis runs a manual 2-4 meeting sales process over 3-6 months with no systematic follow-up between meetings. Sophisticated LPs going dark have no soft re-entry point.

30-50% of warm conversations lost between meeting touchpoints

No retargeting or pixel tracking

No Meta or Google pixel detected on the Thesis website. Visitors who land on the site once and leave never see Thesis again.

The Core Issue
Thesis Capital has the differentiated thesis and the operator-led story to attract sophisticated LPs. The constraint is distribution, not product. Every LP currently in the pipeline came through manual cold outreach or warm intro. A paid acquisition channel layered on top of the placement agent and Connor's cold outbound would compound rather than replace existing efforts. The first priority before running ads is rebuilding the website with an investor-facing section so cold traffic has somewhere to convert.
