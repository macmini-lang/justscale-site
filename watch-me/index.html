Step 2

Then tighten the funnel between the click and the close.

Organic lets you be lazy with your marketing. Half-assed funnels still convert on warm traffic. That is not true of cold. Three components have to be dialed: messaging, pre-call, and sales reps. Get all three right and the same lead flow converts at twice the rate.

Messaging that attracts buyers, not broke prospects

It's not about being different. It's about attracting the right type of person. Slightly off messaging and you fill your calendar with people who can't actually buy. Get the hook, the positioning, and the language across your ads, landing pages, and emails pointing at the same exact problem your real buyer wakes up thinking about, and the calls you take change overnight.

Pre-call material so people actually show up

Confirmation page with prep videos, FAQ, and the calendar in one place. Fastest fix for show rate that exists.

Sales reps who can actually close

Structure on the call. Recordings to review. Follow-up on no-shows and non-closers. The best funnel in the world dies on the call if your reps can't close.
