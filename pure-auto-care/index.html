How long does each service take?
Hand Wash & Interior runs 2-3 hours. Wash & Wax is 3-4 hours. Full Detail takes 4-6 hours. The PURE Package with full paint correction and ceramic coating is a 1-2 day job. The coating needs to cure properly before you drive it.
What's the difference between a sealant and a ceramic coating?
A wax or sealant lasts a few months and sits on top of the paint. A ceramic coating bonds chemically to the clear coat and lasts multiple years. It's the closest thing to permanent protection. It repels water, dirt, and road grime, and makes washing the car dramatically easier. If you plan to keep your car more than a couple of years, coating pays for itself.
Why is the SUV/truck price higher?
More panels, more paint, more interior surface. A full-size truck takes roughly 40% more product and time than a sedan. The price reflects the actual work, not the vehicle you own.
What if my car is really rough? Pet hair, heavy stains, neglected paint?
We'll still take it. If it's beyond what the package covers, we'll send you a quote before starting any extra work. No surprise charges at pickup.
How do I pay?
Card, cash, Venmo, or Zelle. Payment is due when you see the finished car. Not before.
Do you offer gift certificates?
Yes. Text or call us and we'll send one over. A Full Detail makes a genuinely good gift for anyone who cares about their car.
